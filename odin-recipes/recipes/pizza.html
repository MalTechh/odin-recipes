Pizza Recipe

Today we have some fresh pizza straight from italy! We are going to be using a premade crust but other than that everything will be made fresh in your kitchen. We will be making a sauce out of tomatoes, garlic, and oil. We will also be hand-slicing our own pepperoni out of a pepperoni stick.

  • Premade Crust
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic Powder
  • Olive Oil
  • Pepperoni stick
  • Shredded Mozzerela Cheese
  1. Blend together 4 tomatoes, 2 tablespoons of garlic powder, and a tsp of olive oil
  2. Slice 1/3 inch slices of your pepperoni stick until you have enough for your own desire
  3. Spread Premade crust on a round baking sheet and apply the sauce we made in step 1
  4. Put as many pieces of pepperoni as you want and cover with approximately 3/4 cup of mozzerela cheese
  5. Bake in the oven at 375 degrees for 14 minutes or until cheese starts turning golden brown
