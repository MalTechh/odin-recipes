Speghetti Recipe

Today we have a recipe for some delicious speghetti straight from italy. This pasta is made using a four cheese sauce and tomatoes as a base and is spiced up using garlic and paprika.

  • Tomatoes
  • Ragu Four Cheese Sauce
  • Paprika
  • Minced Garlic
  • Half Length Speghetti noodles
  1. Step 1: Blend together tomatoes, paprika, and minced garlic
  2. Step 2: Start boiling 6 cups of water and pour in 8 oz of pasta
  3. Step3: Mix together all blended ingredients with Ragu sauce
  4. Step 4: Strain pasta and mix in with sauce
  5. Enjoy!
